Colin Lee

My passion for data comes from my life-long obsession with numbers. As a kid, I was fascinated with the idea of compiling a player's statistics in order to determine their worth. Now, I am equally fascinated with the concept of using data to gain insights and make decisions. I find that being able to quantify and assign value to ideas through data analysis provides an elevated level of confidence to be able to make decisions. And who doesn't want more confidence to make decisions! So give me some data and let's get started!
